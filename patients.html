Optimising Health

Optimise BP helps your practice look after people with high blood pressure

Working with Optimise BP will help you to control your blood pressure in lots of ways:

Track Your Blood Pressure

Easily monitor your blood pressure with Optimise BP. You can see your last BP reading, your target BP, and your average BP over the last four weeks. This helps you stay in control.

Plan Your Next Steps

Optimise BP provides a clear outline of the next couple of actions you need to take. This helps you plan and prepare.

Manage Your Medication

Keep track of the BP medication you're taking and receive clear instructions on when and how to take your blood pressure, and when to arrange for blood tests.

How Optimise BP Works

If your GP practice has signed up to use Optimise BP, here's how we help:

Identify Hidden High Blood Pressure

Your GP practice may ask you to record your BP, helping to find and manage hidden high blood pressure.

Manage Known High Blood Pressure

Optimise BP helps you and your practice lower your BP by understanding more about your condition. The app saves time and reduces the need for appointments.

Recording Your Blood Pressure

You can record your BP using your own machine, at the GP practice, or in a pharmacy. Your GP can then see these readings and add them to your GP record.




Medication Management

Once your GP has decided on the best medication for you, Optimise BP will:

  • Remind you when to take your BP.
  • Notify you when to have blood tests.
  • Ask you about any side effects.
  • Guide you to safely increase your dose if your BP is still not controlled, following your doctorâ€™s recommendations.

Safety and Support

Optimise BP has built-in safety features to alert you and your practice if any problems arise. You can contact your practice at any time if you have concerns.
